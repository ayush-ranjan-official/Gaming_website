Introduction

Welcome to the Intersection
of the Pet and the Gaming industry.

Furverse, is a free to play First person shooter set in the year 2459 blending thrilling combat with a parallel economy of real world-pet products and pet care.

  1. Saviours Ensemble

    Embark on the mission to rescue sentient Anthropomorphs. Cut through zombies and release as many sentients as you can to scale ranks and unlock new modes.

    Play as your Pet

    Start training your rescued sentients, hone their explosive abilities, equip apparel and skins, and incubate them to transform the virtual character to your own real life pet.

  2. Timeless Artifacts

    Jump portals to visit underworlds dominated by evil foes guarding wealth of immense value and mythic items. Bring them back to trade on the open marketplace.

    Reward Items

    Collect in-game items as you progress in the game. Use them for a varied purpose from increasing your armour and health to charging mythic abilities.

  3. Multiplayer

    Save sentients and move to the second season where you can choose your preferred pet as your gameplay character. Slay your adversaries in tournaments, challenges and maps.

    Pet GPT

    Leverage Ai and ML to get the best product, health advice, medications and assistance for your pet. Purchase preferred pet products right from our marketplace using FURS tokens.

Characters

There will be 5 cat and 5 Dog characters on Furverse. These Anthropomorphs bring their type-3 civilization knowledge to our universe and will use that in heart pounding battles. All characters are NFTs and are based on the ERC1151 standard. Once rescued they can be upgraded and evolved to unleash absolute carnage.
